SPECIALIZED RISK

All too often, many other important lines of coverage fall outside of the scope of a traditional employee benefits package because they are managed by someone within the organization other than Human Resources. These specialized risks include:

  • International Coverage
  • Business Travel Accident
  • Student Accident & Coverage Abroad
  • Voluntary Benefits, and Many More!

If not structured properly, these specialized benefits can create compliance risks both domestic and abroad. There can be redundancy or duplication of coverage with other plans such as Workers Compensation, Foreign Voluntary, or Auto Policies. Failure to protect your company from these complex risk exposures may jeopardize an employer’s duty of care obligations.
RiskFäktor’s Management Risk Division supports the growing demand for specialized and global risk mitigation expertise.

STOP LOSS RECOVERY

Are all your stop loss claims paid?

A significant time lag exists between the date of service and when the claim is processed by your TPA or carrier. For this reason, it’s typical that only 85% of your large loss claims be considered by your stop loss carrier. This 15% of unpaid stop loss claims can add up to tens of thousands of dollars.
RiskFäktor has a solution to ensure that at least 98% of your outstanding excess liability is found and processed by your stop loss carrier.

A prospective specific/individual policy is required and you must engage with RiskFäktor 90 days before the end of your insurance contractual period on either a fee-for-service or percentage of savings basis. Savings can be significant:
